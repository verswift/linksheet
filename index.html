About 
Linksheet is a convenient way to make local browser bookmarks with a minimalist interface. It works by using your browser's local storage so nothing is written to our servers and your privacy is maintained. It can be used for many different things such as for quicklinks to your favourite websites for each of your devices, Reminders and Notifiers with links to the relevant web content and many more. Click the globe to visit the inputted site and the trash can to delete. This is the first release so was kept simple feature wise. If you have any feedback or suggestions for this application, please visit the main website using the verswift icon in the navbar and fill out the contact form on the homepage. Enjoy, and don't forget to check out our other apps.
